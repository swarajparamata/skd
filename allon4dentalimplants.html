All-on-4 Dental Implants

A Full Arch Solution for Your Smile

All-on-4 implants provide a permanent, stable solution for replacing an entire arch of teeth using just four implants. At Smile Keeper Dental, we use advanced technology to deliver natural-looking, functional results.

  • Full arch restoration with minimal implants
  • Immediate functionality and aesthetics
  • Prevents bone loss and supports facial structure
  • Comfortable and durable alternative to dentures

All-on-4 Implants FAQ

All-on-4 implants use four strategically placed implants to support a full arch of teeth, offering a stable, permanent solution.

Performed under anesthesia, the procedure is comfortable, with most patients reporting minimal discomfort during recovery.

With proper care, All-on-4 implants can last 20+ years, often a lifetime, with regular dental checkups.

Most adults with significant tooth loss or inadequate bone for traditional implants are candidates. A consultation will confirm eligibility.
