Denture Relines FAQ

A denture reline adds a new layer of material to the inner surface of your denture to improve fit and comfort without replacing the entire base.

Hard relines use durable material for long-term fit, while soft relines provide cushioning for sensitive gums, often used temporarily or for comfort.

A reline typically takes 1-2 appointments, with same-day service possible for some cases, depending on the type of reline.

Relines are typically needed every 1-2 years or when dentures become loose due to changes in your gums or jawbone.
