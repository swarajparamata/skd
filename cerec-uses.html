Uses of CEREC Technology

Versatile Restorations in One Visit

CEREC technology at Smile Keeper Dental enables same-day fabrication of high-quality ceramic restorations, including crowns, inlays, onlays, and veneers, tailored to your needs.

  • Crowns for damaged or decayed teeth
  • Inlays and onlays for partial tooth restoration
  • Veneers for aesthetic smile enhancement
  • Quick, precise restorations with no temporary solutions
